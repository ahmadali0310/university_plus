Sarhad Institute of EMERGING Technologies
Joint Venture of SUIT & CIT

My dear university graduates of Pakistan((Employed and underemployed) you already know that the recent digital transformation has dramatically changed the learning paradigm especially in emerging technologies e.g. IoT, BlockChain, AI, Digital Architecture, and many more. During 3-5 years all those University graduates who have formally completed their degrees are either unemployed or underemployed. Most of them have not joined the carriers of their formal educational degrees. The reason is that due to fast digital transformation and pace of dynamic change in our educational policy and strategy could not maintain the compatibility with the fast change. Center of Information Technology (CIT) limited company established 25 years ago having a vast experience of University education and imparting training ten10, 000 of university graduated (Allama Iqbal Open University and Sarhad University) has started the initiative University plus which aims to bring the existing gap in demanding education based in the industry needs today and the existing system of education in universities. For this purpose, CIT has engaged 50+ Ph.D. Doctors and domain experts in emerging technologies to start three months of short certification courses backed by their international accreditation and leading university. I welcome all intended trainees to join the program University Plus based on the details on the website.
